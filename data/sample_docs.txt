Contoso Electronics Return Policy

Contoso Electronics accepts returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in their original condition and packaging. A receipt or proof of purchase is required for all returns.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a defect or our error.

The following items are not eligible for return: opened software, downloadable products, gift cards, and customised or special-order items.

---

Contoso Electronics Warranty

All Contoso Electronics products come with a 12-month limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. The warranty does not cover damage from accidents, misuse, or unauthorised modifications.

To make a warranty claim, contact our support team at support@contoso.example with your order number and a description of the issue. We will replace defective units at no cost within the warranty period.

Extended warranty plans are available for purchase within 90 days of buying your product.

---

Contoso Electronics Shipping Policy

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50. Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available for an additional $12.99.

We currently ship to the continental United States, Alaska, and Hawaii. International shipping is not available at this time.

Orders placed before 2pm EST on business days are processed the same day. Orders placed after 2pm or on weekends are processed the next business day.
